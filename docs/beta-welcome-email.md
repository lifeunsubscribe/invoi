## Getting Started

Here's how to create your first invoice:

1. **Set up your profile** - Add your name, contact info, hourly rate, and client details. This information will appear on all your invoices.

2. **Create a weekly invoice** - Enter the hours you worked each day this week. Choose from seven beautiful designer templates (Morning Light, Coastal, Garden, and more).

3. **Send it to your client** - Email your invoice directly from Invoi. We'll track when it's sent, paid, or overdue.

4. **View your history** - Browse all your invoices in a calendar view. Export monthly reports for your accountant at tax time.

**Access Invoi:** https://goinvoi.com
